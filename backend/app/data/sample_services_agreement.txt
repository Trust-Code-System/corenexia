MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of March 1, 2026 (the
"Effective Date") by and between Meridian Capital Advisors, LLC, a Delaware limited liability
company with offices at 200 Corporate Plaza, Wilmington, Delaware ("Client"), and Lattice
Analytics Inc., a Delaware corporation with offices at 55 Market Street, San Francisco,
California ("Provider").

1. TERM. This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial term
of twenty-four (24) months, unless earlier terminated in accordance with Section 7. Thereafter,
the Agreement shall automatically renew for successive twelve (12) month periods.

2. SERVICES. Provider shall furnish financial data analysis and equity research support
services as described in one or more Statements of Work executed by the parties.

3. FEES. Client shall pay Provider an annual fee of USD 480,000, invoiced quarterly in advance.

7. TERMINATION. Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days'
prior written notice to the other party. Either party may terminate immediately upon a material
breach that remains uncured thirty (30) days after written notice.

11. GOVERNING LAW. This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws
of the State of Delaware, without regard to its conflict-of-laws principles.

12. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY. In no event shall either party's aggregate liability exceed the
total fees paid by Client in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.
